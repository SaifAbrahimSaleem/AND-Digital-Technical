This section contains a wealth of information, related to Golden Shoe Store and its store. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us via email: support@goldenshoe.co.uk, our hotline: 07123456789, or alternatively, contact us via our contact form. We respond to all contact as soon as we can (9am-4pm Monday-Friday).

General Questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Golden Shoe Store is highly advisable but not necessary. Registering an account will help to keep track of existing orders, as well as pending orders.

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign-up" button. Once clicked, you will be prompted with a form to fill in. Once completely filled in, click the "Create Account" button. A confirmation email will be sent to the provided email address to activate the account.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Login" button. Once clicked, you will be prompted with a login form for which, click the "forgot password" button and enter the email address associated with the account.

Orders

Do you provide Tracking information?

Once you have placed an order, you will receive a confirmation email, detailing your order, along with your order reference. Once your order has been submitted for shipping, you will receive a further email detailing your tracking information

Can I make changes to my order?

As soon as orders have been received, as long as your order has not been submitted for processing, we can provide assistance in changing your order. If your order is currently being, or has already been processed, unfortunately, you cannot change your order.

Can I make cancel my order?

As soon as orders have been received, as long as your order has not been submitted for processing, we can provide assistance in cancelling your order. If your order is currently being, or has already been processed, unfortunately, you cannot cancel your order.

Can I make changes to the shipping address in my order?

As soon as orders have been received, as long as your order has not been submitted for processing, we can provide assistance in changing the shipping address of your order. If your order is currently being, or has already been processed, unfortunately, you cannot change the shipping address of your order.

What is the process for making returns?

The process for initiating returns of items is easy! Please refer to our Returns Policy for more information regarding our returns.

How much is shipping?

Shipping rates vary based on your location and the size of your order. The easiest method of estimating your shipping rate would be to check the amount via the checkout page.

Do you provide any discounts?

We offer many forms of discounts! Look out for any of the following:
  1. Discount Vouchers sent via email
  2. Online Shopping Schemes such as Honey/Coupon Mate
  3. Holiday discounts on selected items
